Semester or monthly — which should I choose?

Semester is a one-time payment covering 5 months — perfect for exam season. The IGCSE semester (LE 1,400) saves LE 350 compared to 5 monthly payments. Monthly is best if you're unsure how long you need access.

What is the Gemini Pro credit system?

Each plan includes a monthly allowance of Gemini Pro credits — the most capable model tier. When Pro credits run out, you automatically continue on Gemini Flash at no extra cost. If you regularly need more Pro credits, just contact us on Telegram and we'll work something out.

Is this just ChatGPT with a different name?

No. Each mentor is built on a deeply specialized system that knows your specific syllabus, exam traps, mark scheme language, and question patterns from 2016–2025. Generic AI cannot do this.

How do I pay and get access?

Payment via InstaPay or Vodafone Cash — no card needed. Contact us on Telegram, pay, and receive your login credentials within minutes.

What exam boards are covered?

IGCSE mentors cover Cambridge (CAIE) and Pearson Edexcel. Thanaweya mentors cover the Egyptian Ministry curriculum, El-Moasser aligned. Cambridge 0971 students — same syllabus as 0620, fully supported.

What is the Anki Architect?

A memory tool that converts any mentor explanation into Anki flashcards using exact mark scheme language. Flags trap points, recommends settings for your exam timeline. Included free with every plan.
